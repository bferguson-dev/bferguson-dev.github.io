About

Professional focus

My work is centered on infrastructure tasks that are usually documented but still handled manually: remediation steps, compliance checks, workstation setup, connectivity validation, and repeatable lab operations. I am most interested in roles where that kind of work can be turned into durable internal tooling.

The projects here are all presented conservatively. Where a tool has strong unit coverage but limited production validation, the site says so directly. That is intentional and part of how I approach engineering communication.
